Homemade Mac and Cheese

Serves four

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package of macaroni
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 cups of shredded low-fat Cheddar cheese
  • 0.5 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups of 1% fat milk (low fat)
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 0.5 cup of bread crumbs
  • 1 pinch of paprika

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Bring a large pot of (salted) water to boil
  3. Cook your macaroni in the boiling water for 8 minutes or until desires firmness, then drain
  4. Use a saucepan to melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat
  5. Add and stir in flour to make a roux
  6. Add milk to your roux, stirring constantly
  7. Stir in the parmesand and cheddar and cook over low heat for 3 minutes
  8. Place macaroni in a baking dish then add your sauce over it, stirring well
  9. Melt 2 tablespoons of butter in a skillet over medium heat
  10. Add breadcrumbs, and stir until the butter is absorbed (will take 2-3 minutes)
  11. Spread your breadcrumbs over the macaroni, and sprinkle with paprike
  12. Bake in your preheated oven for about 30 minutes
  13. Enjoy! Your mac and cheese is ready to serve!
