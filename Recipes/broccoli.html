Broccoli Fritters

Serves eight

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of Dijon mustard
  • 0.25 (1/4) cup of shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of minced fresh chives, plus more for garnish
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 0.5 teaspoon of freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 cups of chopped cooked broccoli
  • 0.5 cup of shredded carrots
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil

Directions

  1. Add eggs and mustard into a bowl together, stir till well-blended
  2. Add parmesan cheese, chives, grlic, salt, and pepper to bowl
  3. Add your chopped broccoli and shredded carrots to the bowl, and stir until ingredients are evenly mixed together
  4. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat
  5. Divie your vegetable mixture into 8 equal portions; shaped them into 3 inch patties
  6. Add your patties to the hot oil of the skillet for 3-4 minutes or until brown
  7. Reduce your heat toe medium-low, and turn your patties
  8. Cook until brown for another 3-4 minutes
  9. Place the patties into a warm serving platter, and add garnish
  10. Enjoy! Ready to eat
