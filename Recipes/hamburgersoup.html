Hamburger Soup

Serves 8

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 (14.5 ounce) cans beef consomme
  • 1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed tomato soup
  • 4 carrots, finely chopped
  • 3 stalks celery, finely chopped
  • 4 tablespoons pearl barley
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf

You will need a multi-functional pressure cooker (like an Instant Pot)

Directions

  1. Turn on your Instant Pot (or other pressure cooker type) and turn to saute function
  2. Throw in beef and onions, and cook them until they brown (Should take 5-10 minutes)
  3. Add the beef consomme, tomatoes, water, and tomato soup to your pressure cooker
  4. Add carrots, celery, barley, thyme, and bay leaf
  5. Close and lock the lid of the pressure cooker
  6. Select the soup function on your cooker, and set timer for 30 minutes, allowing 10-15 minutes for pressure to build
  7. Release pressure using the natural-release method, about 10 minutes
  8. Ready to serve!
